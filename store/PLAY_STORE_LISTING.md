## Full Description

Maxxed Compass combines an outdoor compass, local trip tracker, and offline sky guide in one focused Android app.

Switch between magnetic and true north, monitor calibration confidence and possible magnetic interference, and use a clear compass dial in daylight or night mode. Start a walking or trail session to track distance, duration, speed, elevation change, segments, and local trip history. Pause, resume, or stop an active trip from its persistent notification.

Sky Scanner uses your device time, location, and orientation to calculate the positions of supported stars, constellations, the Moon, and planets. Use the red map for dark-adapted viewing or place the calculated guidance over an optional live camera preview. Sky Scanner is a directional aid, not camera plate solving or professional astronomy equipment.

Features:

- Magnetic and true-north compass
- Calibration and interference guidance
- Metric and imperial units
- Foreground trip tracking with notification controls
- Local trip history, names, and segments
- Calculated offline sky map with all 88 selectable constellations, dots, connecting lines, and search
- Optional camera preview
- Night mode and display controls
- No account, ads, analytics, or Internet permission

Compass and GPS accuracy depend on device hardware, calibration, surroundings, and sky visibility. Keep the phone away from magnets and metal when taking a bearing. Do not use this app as the sole navigation or safety tool.
