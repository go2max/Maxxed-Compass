## Full description

Maxxed Compass is an offline Android compass built for practical field use. Check magnetic or true-north heading, track local trips, review saved history, use night-friendly display options, and open a calculated sky guide without creating an account.

Core features:

- Magnetic and true-north compass headings
- Sensor confidence and magnetic-interference guidance
- Local trip tracking with pause, resume, stop, segments, and history
- Metric and imperial units
- Night mode, themes, and keep-screen-on controls
- Offline Sky Scanner with selectable constellations, red map, camera preview, and search
- Local-first storage for settings, trips, history, and sky preferences

Privacy posture:

Maxxed Compass does not request Internet access. Trip, setting, location, camera, and sensor processing is designed to stay on the device unless you choose to export or share information outside the app.

Important limitations:

- Compass accuracy depends on phone sensors, calibration, magnetic interference, and field conditions.
- Sky Scanner uses calculated positions from device time, location, and orientation. It is not cloud recognition or certified navigation.
- Maxxed Compass is not a certified marine, aviation, emergency, or land-survey instrument.
